35 years looking to the future!
Created in 1986 by Thierry Dreux, a dental technician, Orthoplus is a French SME, supplier of global solutions in orthodontics for practitioners.
Initially a specialist in made-to-measure multi-brackets, the company has been developing a new therapeutic approach since the 1990s: Functional Education.
